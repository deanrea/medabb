NURSING MEDICAL ABBREVIATION

These medical abbreviations are commonly found on patient charts and other official records. You can find nurses using these abbreviations in hospitals, at doctor's offices, at blood drives, in nursing homes, in clinics, and when educating the public. These terminologies are important for us to have a more understandable and efficient communication.
